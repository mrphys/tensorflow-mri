Coil Arrays¶

Coil array operations.

This module contains functions to operate with MR coil arrays, such as estimating coil sensitivities and combining multi-coil images.

coil_compression_matrix

Calculate a coil compression matrix.

combine_coils

Sum of squares or adaptive coil combination.

compress_coils

Coil compression gateway.

estimate_coil_sensitivities

Estimate coil sensitivity maps.
