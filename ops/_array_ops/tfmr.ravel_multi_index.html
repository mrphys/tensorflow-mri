tfmr.ravel_multi_index¶

tfmr.ravel_multi_index(multi_index, dims)[source]¶

Converts an array of multi-indices into an array of flat indices.

Parameters

  • multi_index – A Tensor of shape [..., N] containing multi-indices into an N-dimensional tensor.

  • dims – A Tensor of shape [N]. The shape of the tensor that multi_index indexes into.

Returns

A Tensor of shape [...] containing flat indices equivalent to multi_index.
