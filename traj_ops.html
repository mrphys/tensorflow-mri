k-space trajectories¶

k-space trajectory operations.

This module contains functions to operate with k-space trajectories, such as calculation of trajectories and sampling density.

radial_trajectory

Calculate a radial trajectory.

spiral_trajectory

Calculate a spiral trajectory.

radial_waveform

Calculate a radial readout waveform.

spiral_waveform

Calculate a spiral readout waveform.

radial_density

Calculate sampling density for radial trajectories.

estimate_density

Estimate the density of an arbitrary set of points.
